who am i

I'm Jacob, an award-winning stylist with over 21 years of Experience in South Korea, The UK, Japan And Australia.

I specialises in ladies and mens cutting and styling, as well as restyling, setting, colouring/foiling. hair rejuvenation, treatment, advice on haircare, extentions as well as styling for events such as formals and bridal parties.
